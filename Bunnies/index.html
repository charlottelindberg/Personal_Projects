Find a partner for your special bunny!

Bunnies are by nature very social animals. In the wild they live in large warrens with several generations, taking care of each other. The need for company does not change for bunnies in captivity. To prevent your bunny from becoming depressed (and effectivly shortening their lifespan by several years - yes a bunny can die from loneliness and depression), we will help you and your special little bunny to find a friend to share their lives with.
